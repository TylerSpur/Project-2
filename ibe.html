Our Story and History

Welcome to Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven, a premier destination for hang-gliding and skydiving enthusiasts located in the heart of Indiana. Our journey began in 2010, driven by a passion for the skies and a commitment to safety and exhilaration.

Our founders, Harry and Harriet, have cultivated a thriving community of adventure seekers. We're proud to offer:

  • Comprehensive training programs
  • State-of-the-art equipment sales
  • Personalized adventure packages
