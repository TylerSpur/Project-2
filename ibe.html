Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven

Our Story and History

Founded in the heart of Indiana, Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven has been the premier destination for hang-gliding and skydiving enthusiasts since its inception in 2010. What started as a small, passionate venture by co-founders Harry and Harriet has grown into a thriving haven for adventure seekers. Our mission is to provide the safest, most exhilarating hang-gliding and skydiving experiences for people of all skill levels. From humble beginnings, we've expanded our services to include comprehensive training programs, state-of-the-art equipment sales, and personalized adventure packages, all while maintaining our core values of safety, community, and fun.
